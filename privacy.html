Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

We do not collect sensitive personal information, financial data, or precise location data.

Mobile App & Permissions

If you use AR scanning, the app will request access to your device’s camera. Camera input is used to estimate dimensions for your space. Images are processed for this feature and are not sold or shared for advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide and improve core features (such as scanning and personalised suggestions), measure performance and engagement, and—if you opt in—send updates and announcements. We will never sell your data to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely. We use trusted third-party providers such as Supabase (for secure backend storage and authentication), Netlify (for website hosting), and email marketing platforms (e.g., Mailchimp) to manage and store your data. Access is restricted and protected.

Your Rights

You may unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in our messages, or by contacting us at hello@tidea.app. You can also request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies for analytics and performance tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time.

This helps support the ongoing development of TIDEA and allows us to continue offering tools and inspiration at no extra cost to you.
